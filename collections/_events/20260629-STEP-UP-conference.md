Building on a record‑attendance 2025 event, this year’s STEP-UP RSLondon community conference on 29 June 2026 brings digital Research Technical Professionals together from across London and the South East. The conference includes plenary sessions on cross-cutting dRTP themes (particuarly career pathways and the impact of LLMs) and parallel tracks on research software, research data and research computing infrastructure. We will have talks, lightning talks and posters focused on skills, practice and collaboration across the dRTP landscape. 

#### Submissions and registration
**Call for abstracts**: regular talks (15+5 mins), lightning talks (5 mins), and A1 posters.  
**Deadlines**: Abstracts due Tuesday 14 Aprril 2026 (23:59 BST); notifications by Tuesday 19 May 2026; registration closes Friday 12 June 2026.  
**Registration**: opening soon.

[Click here for full details of the event and how to submit](https://step-up.ac.uk/events/step-up-2026/)
